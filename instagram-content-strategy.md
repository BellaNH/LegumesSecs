## üìÖ Content Mix Strategy

### Weekly Posting Schedule

| Day | Content Type | Purpose |
|-----|-------------|---------|
| **Monday** | Problem-Solution Post | Start week with value, address pain points |
| **Tuesday** | Technical Tip/Quick Tutorial | Show expertise, educate audience |
| **Wednesday** | Portfolio Showcase | Demonstrate capabilities, inspire |
| **Thursday** | Educational/Industry Insight | Build authority, provide value |
| **Friday** | Behind-the-Scenes | Humanize brand, show personality |
| **Saturday** | Client Testimonial or Case Study | Build trust, social proof |
| **Sunday** | Promotion or Engagement Post | Drive conversions, increase engagement |

### Content Ratio Guidelines
- **Value Content**: 80% (educational, helpful, entertaining)
- **Promotional Content**: 20% (offers, services, CTAs)
